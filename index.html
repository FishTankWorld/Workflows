Watch Outs
Risks, ethical considerations and implementation traps
LinkedIn Terms of Service Risk
Multi-account automation tools violate LinkedIn's ToS and risk account suspension. FishTank's position: work within LinkedIn's limits intelligently, not at unsafe volume.
GDPR on Automated Outreach
Automated email to scraped lists requires legitimate interest basis under UK GDPR. FishTank must build compliant templates and ensure clients understand their legal basis.
CRM Data Quality Dependency
All AI tools are only as good as the CRM data beneath them. Every sprint needs a CRM hygiene phase before automation is layered on top.
AI Replacing Human Relationships
Recruitment is a relationship business. AI handles the admin — humans own the conversation. Never automate the call itself, only everything around it.

Watch Outs
AI Bias in Shortlisting
AI matching trained on historical placement data can perpetuate bias — favouring candidates who look like past successful placements. Every AI shortlist needs a human review layer and a bias governance policy.
Fake CV Detection
AI-generated CVs are flooding the market in 2026. Agencies need AI tools that detect fabricated credentials (hireEZ, Bullhorn AI) built into the shortlisting step — not an afterthought.
GDPR Candidate Data
Candidate records in Bullhorn without active consent or legitimate interest are a ticking clock. Sourcing automation that adds new contacts must trigger a consent workflow immediately.
CRM Data Decay
B2B data decays at 22.5% annually. Candidate data even faster. AI matching against stale records produces garbage. Data hygiene automation is a prerequisite, not an optional extra.
